About Lelouch

Lelouch vi Britannia is the main protagonist of Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion and Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion R2, and plays a supporting role in sidestories like Code Geass: Oz the Reflection and Code Geass: Lancelot & Guren. After he was exiled, he used the alias, Lelouch Lamperouge. He was the Eleventh Prince of the Holy Britannian Empire and the son of the 98th Emperor of Britannia, Charles zi Britannia. He was also the leader and founder of The Black Knights and the real identity of Zero.

STRENGTHS:


GEASS

Geass allows him to plant commands within a person's mind upon eye contact like hypnosis.

INTELLIGENCE

Lelouch's greatest weapon is his prodigious mind, possessing a genius-level intellect.

DIPLOMACY

Lelouch is capable of devising and executing strategies with incredible speed and precision.
